Q&A

Is scuba diving safe?

Scuba diving can be a safe and rewarding activity when done properly. Here at Yanny diving company, safety is our top priority. We have certified instructors who will guide you through every step of the process.

Do I need experience to go scuba diving?

No prior experience is necessary to try scuba diving! We offer introductory dives (also known as Discover Scuba Diving) that allow you to experience the underwater world for the first time in a safe and controlled environment.

What kind of marine life can I expect to see while scuba diving?

The marine life you encounter depends on the specific dive location. However, you can expect to see a variety of amazing creatures!
